FAQ

1. What are the possible side effects of Digene Acidity and Gas Relief Tablet?

These tablets are usually safe and do not cause any side effects when taken as per the doctor's recommendation. However, if your symptoms do not improve or if your condition worsens, consult your doctor immediately.

2. Can I take Digene if I am pregnant?

It is wise to consult your gynaecologist before taking this medicine if you are pregnant. Similarly, breastfeeding women should also consult the doctor before consumption.

3. How can I prevent acidity?

You must avoid excess intake of oily, spicy and salty foods as well as carbonated drinks. Try to eat small, frequent meals and opt for a low-carbohydrate and high fibre diet. Limit your intake of alcohol and caffeine. Avoid eating late at night, do not exercise or sleep immediately after meals and keep a check on your weight and drink lots of water.
